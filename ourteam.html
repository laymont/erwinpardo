ERWIN PARDO

Photographer/Director

Erwin is a commercial photographer and filmmaker, with more than 20 years of experience, he has dedicated his work to product, corporate, gastronomic, architecture and portrait photography, in his career he has had the opportunity to participate in commercial projects of recognized chains hotels and exclusive furniture brands and luxury restaurants around the world, as well as clothing by famous designers. More recently, he participated in the Sea Island Resort Hotel campaign, collaborating with award-winning international photographer Antonio Cuellar and fellow celebrity photographer with extensive experience, Elio Escalante. Erwin is also a drone operator licensed in the United States by the FAA.

His photographic career has passed in parallel with the creation of audiovisual pieces, his experience in film dates back to the times of analog cinema, now in digital times he has adapted so that technology together with knowledge, provides his clients with the best results.

Erwin truly enjoys being involved in audiovisual, photographic and film projects, to the point of imbuing him with passion until turning his work into a lasting and effective final finish.

ELIO ESCALANTE

Photographer

Elio Escalante is a multifaceted photographer, who has built his experience in the world of commercial photography for more than 20 years, collaborating with large advertising agencies, as well as the most important media in Miami.

In parallel, he has worked in the world of fashion, bringing to the public eye the creations of prominent haute couture designers, as well as great personalities from the world of music.

More recently, he was in charge of all the audiovisual production of the singer Oscar D'Leon, in a world tour that took him to a large part of Europe, where he was able to capture the emotion of the public and the interactivity with the artist.

Elio Escalante is a prominent photographer with extensive experience who adds his enthusiasm and passion for photography to this audiovisual team, whose goal is to provide our clients and partners with the highest quality material that meets the objectives set.
