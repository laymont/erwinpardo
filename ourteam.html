ERWIN PARDO

Photographer-Director

Erwin Pardo is a seasoned commercial photographer and filmmaker with over two decades of experience in capturing high-quality images and creating captivating audiovisual content. His portfolio boasts an impressive range of specialties, including product, corporate, gastronomic, architecture, and portrait photography. Throughout his illustrious career, he has worked on numerous commercial projects for renowned hotels, exclusive furniture brands, luxury restaurants, and famous designers. Notably, he was involved in the Sea Island Resort Hotel campaign, collaborating with internationally acclaimed photographer Antonio Cuellar and highly experienced celebrity photographer Elio Escalante. Additionally, Erwin is an FAA licensed drone operator in the United States, holding a degree in Social Communication and an Associate in Advertising.

Erwin's passion for photography is evident in his enthusiasm for audiovisual and cinematographic projects, which he approaches with a meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to excellence. He seamlessly blends traditional analogue techniques with modern digital technologies to produce top-notch results that exceed his clients' expectations.

ELIO ESCALANTE

Photographer

Elio Escalante is a versatile and experienced photographer with over two decades of experience in commercial photography. He has collaborated with major advertising agencies and media outlets in Miami, Florida, and has worked in the fashion industry, showcasing the creations of top haute couture designers and music personalities. Recently, he oversaw the entire audiovisual production of Salsa singer Oscar D'Leon's world tour, capturing the audience's emotions and interaction with the artist.

As an integral member of the audiovisual team, Elio brings his passion and expertise to every project, ensuring that the final product meets and exceeds the clients' objectives. His extensive experience and enthusiasm for photography make him a valuable addition to the team.

ALBERTO MAUCÓ

Business Development

Alberto Maucó is a highly regarded and accomplished Producer, Presenter, Public Relations, and Writer based in Miami. He is an expert in corporate image and negotiation, with an excellent understanding of luxury, good taste, and hospitality. His involvement in audiovisual projects is total, and he works tirelessly to set ambitious goals for every project that the team takes on. His in-depth knowledge of art and comfort ensures that the team produces exciting and visually stunning audiovisual pieces that meet the clients' objectives.
